## Why

YY has been with Ben for 40 years. Every entry that includes YY documents both of them — a 40-year-old stuffed squirrel and the human who has carried him since childhood. That documentation is not incidental. It is structural. The invariants guarantee it.

Precursor: 300 YouTube videos, 16 million views. Practice logs in public. That body of work already demonstrated what provenance at scale looks like — scars visible, timeline intact, reasoning traceable. YY's World is the same posture applied to a different medium.

The genealogical purpose requires no announcement. The 6 invariants produce it automatically. Compression preserves the core. Scars preserve the failures. Survivability ensures the record outlasts the moment. Explainability makes it legible to future readers — including grandchildren. Timestamping anchors every artifact in its historical position. Discipline keeps the whole thing from drifting.

A kid reading a YY entry gets a creative artifact. A family member reading the same entry decades later gets a genealogical record. The invariants make both readings simultaneously available without the surface form trying to be both.

## Why-Not

**Why not keep a separate private journal?**
A private record survives only as long as the medium and the owner's control over it. A public record with strong invariants survives independently. The genealogical artifact must be structurally capable of outlasting the operator.

**Why not call it a memoir or autobiography?**
Those are deliberate genres with editorial conventions — narrative arc, selective emphasis, retrospective framing. A genealogical artifact produced by the YY Method has no retrospective framing. Each entry is a snapshot from its moment, timestamped, with scars intact. That is structurally different from memoir.

**Why not separate the kids' content from the life record?**
The dual purpose is the point. The same artifact that is legible to a kid is legible to a future descendant. Separating the surfaces would require each to do less work and would eliminate the coincidence that makes the genealogical record authentic rather than curated.
