# PM-001 — This Is for My Kids First, Everything Else Second

**Status:** museum
**Era:** pre-manifest (before v0.1 schema)
**Superseded by:** YYBW-001 (Product Thesis), Manifest v0.1

---

## Context

There is a strong desire to leave something behind that children can discover whenever they are ready — not necessarily now, maybe 10–20 years later. This is not just content, but a trace of thinking.

## Decision / Direction

Prioritize long-term legibility of thought over short-term usefulness or polish.

## Tensions / Contradictions

- Could conflict with making it interesting for others
- Could bias system toward introspection over exploration

## Implications

System must preserve intent, not just output.
