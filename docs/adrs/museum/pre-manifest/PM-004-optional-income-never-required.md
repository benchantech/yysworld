# PM-004 — Optional Income, Never Required Income

**Status:** museum
**Era:** pre-manifest (before v0.1 schema)
**Superseded by:** YYBW-016 (Distribution), YYBW-017 (Launch Infrastructure)

---

## Context

There is interest in potential monetization, but strong resistance to dependency.

## Decision / Direction

System should support monetization as optional, not required.

## Tensions / Contradictions

- Optimization for income could corrupt intent
- Lack of monetization may limit expansion

## Implications

Design must not assume revenue.

## Notes

"100% fun income"
