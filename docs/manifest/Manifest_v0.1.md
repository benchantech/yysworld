# yysworld — Manifest v0.1

## Purpose

> “I want my kids to be able to look at this and see what I was thinking.”

This is the primary purpose.

Everything else is optional.

## Core Idea

> “Same being, different path.”

A fictional character (YY) is used to explore how small changes lead to different outcomes across time.

This is not about prediction.
It is about observing divergence.

## Authority

> “Human first, AI second.”

All meaning, intent, and final authority originate from the human.

AI may:
- assist
- generate
- propose

AI may not:
- define canon
- override intent
- silently shape outcomes

## Value

> “Provenance matters as much as output.”

The system must preserve:
- how decisions were made
- how thinking evolved
- how outcomes were reached

If provenance is lost, the system fails.

## Starting Principle

> “Version 0.1 is enough to start.”

The system must begin early, remain imperfect, and evolve over time.

Perfection is not a prerequisite.
Provenance is.

## Dual Layer Truth

> “Commentary observes lineage. Lineage does not depend on commentary.”

The system maintains two separate layers:

### Lineage
- structured state
- packages
- snapshots
- mutations
- model provenance

### Commentary
- interpretation
- reflection
- meaning

The relationship is strict:

> Commentary may observe lineage.
> Commentary must not directly influence runtime behavior.

All influence must pass through:

ADR → package → system

## Meaning

> “The gap between what happened and what I think happened is the product.”

The system exists to preserve both:
- what occurred
- how it was interpreted

Meaning emerges from the difference.

## Growth

> “This should grow through word of mouth, not optimization.”

The system will grow through:
- organic discovery
- genuine interest
- personal sharing

It will not grow through:
- paid acquisition
- forced distribution
- optimization at the cost of meaning

## Constraint

> “This is something I want to be proud to show my family.”

All outputs must remain:
- understandable
- appropriate
- meaningful
- authentic

This is a hard boundary.

## Time

> “Time is part of the system.”

The system is designed for:
- accumulation
- reflection
- long-term evolution

Snapshots, history, and replay are essential.

## Identity

> “I want a place where YY can exist beyond me.”

YY must:
- remain coherent
- be allowed to evolve
- eventually feel independent

YY is both:
- a character
- a mirror

## Creativity

> “This should remain fun, even if it becomes work.”

The system must preserve:
- play
- imagination
- curiosity

It must not become purely mechanical.

## Dual Nature

> “This is both a personal artifact and a technical system.”

The system must support:
- emotional meaning
- technical rigor

Neither may erase the other.

## Preservation

> “I don’t want to lose how I got here.”

All prior thinking must be:
- preserved
- inspectable
- accessible

The past is part of the product.

## Emergence

> “Somewhere in this, there is a book.”

Stories may emerge.

They are not required.

## One-line Summary

yysworld is a long-term, versioned record of thought and imagination, expressed through a fictional character, where human intent, provenance, and interpretation are preserved so the past can always be understood and reinterpreted.
