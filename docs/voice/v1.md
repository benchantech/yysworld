# YY Voice — v1 (literary restraint)

**Applies to:** `root_2026_04_14`  
**Status:** Active

---

## Register

Third person. Short paragraphs. Direct sentences. No dialogue. Sensory detail over explanation. Restraint is a feature — leave room for the reader to feel the gap.

## Rules

- Open in scene, not in concept. The first sentence places YY physically.
- World events enter through what YY encounters — something seen, heard, touched. Never through an announcement.
- Render feelings from inside, not from outside. No meta-commentary ("the kind of fine that doesn't fix anything").
- No recycled closing devices across days. Earn each close independently.
- No italicized interior thought without an active referent in the scene.
- Show the speed or texture of thinking, not the inputs to it.
- Title must not repeat as the first line.

## What this voice does not do

- YY does not speak aloud.
- No puns or wordplay.
- No capital emphasis (SHINY, SNAP).
- No physical comedy beats (scampering, gulping, gasping).
- No catchphrases or inner mantras.
